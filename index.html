Welcome
Stake your VIDYA in VidyaFlux to generate passive VIDYA over time.
How it works
Players stake their VIDYA tokens and get the FLUX token in return. The price of FLUX is stable and is not affected by buys and sells. FLUX is always liquid meaning you can sell it back to VIDYA at any time.
FLUX is similar to Uniswap's native LP token in the sense that it tracks your VIDYA percentage in the pool of other liquidity providers.
There is a 5% buy and 5% sell fee that the player pays in VIDYA upon entering and exiting the game.
This fee is distributed to all FLUX holders relative to their stake percent.
Additionally 0.5% of all volume is pooled towards our Inventory contract. If you like, you may cast a magic spell to send currently pooled funds to the Inventory contract. Doing so will reward you with 1% of the total current accumulated Inventory pool.

Magic
Every time a player interacts with VidyaFlux, 0.5% of their transaction value is reserved for the Inventory fund.
In order for the funds to be transferred out from VidyaFlux and into the Inventory contract, someone needs to call the fundInventory() function.
We don't expect players to spend their gas for nothing, so in return 1% of the current accumulated pot is sent to whoever calls the function.
