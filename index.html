What is Vidya

Vidya is a DeFi gaming token and escrow solution for multiplayer player-versus-player games, wagering, and esports betting. The medium of exchange for games and platforms that use VIDYA is a liquid, market-backed ERC-20 token, uniquely also named VIDYA. Through in-game and on-chain mechanics, VIDYA enjoys the unique position of providing entertainment and finance wrapped together as one to an emergent market of decentralized products and services. Decentralized gaming and finance are massive and growing markets with few notable attempts at an innovative merge of industries.

How it works

With open source, decentralized software hosted on the Ethereum blockchain in the form of smart contracts as escrow for players' wagers, holding them upon deposit and releasing them upon completion of prerequisite conditions that are agreed to on entry. Each session is subject to a rake from the pool that's distributed among VIDYA stakers, who will receive rewards proportional to the amount of tokens they've deposited and held within the staking platform. Games will be able to implement our immutable intermediary at ease, with the requirement of interacting with the contracts via Web3 user interface and the use of VIDYA tokens.

System in practice

Player created lobbies are made by Web3 enabled users after they pick the deposit other users must make before being able to join their lobby. The user will then be charged this amount in order to start the lobby, securing the initial wager instantly, and the rest upon entry of other players. Users will be refunded in the event of a lobby closure. Staking fees will only ever be deducted from the pool following successful match completion.

The DeFi angle

Our aesthetic nuance and love of designing games doesn't count us out of financial experimentation, it only gives us the chance to do it with style. We present new ways of designing an interconnected and highly interactable environment for our users. Our Vidya token is advantaged by providing unique markets and tokenomic models within our products and games via the creative utilization of deflationary mechanics, non-fungibility, item degredation and token staking all easily accessbile to you from a single personalized hub, TeamOS.

TeamOS

TeamOS refers to the team3d.io website and delivers all things Team3D through its user interface. You can check out your holdings, stats, cross-game inventory and a plethora of information unique to the public key you use to connect with TeamOS. You can play our games with other users, check market data, buy and sell VIDYA and keep up with new additions to the project.

The year ahead

It took us a little while longer, but we are now getting very close to releasing our first video game- a First Person Shooter. Realistically we can expect a demo launch in late Q1, early Q2.

Strategic partnerships are another aspect we will focus on a lot this year. We are currently in the early days of potentially partnering up with some very reputable crypto companies who share our vision and if all goes well, we might even get extra hands to help us!

Merchant & Astronaut

With the NFT distributor contract and Inventory system completed, we will now focus on the Merchant and Astronaut systems. In simple terms the Merchant will act as a global NFT intermediary for all of games as well as all of the potential games our partners and community members will build on TeamOS. The Astronaut will essentially be a showcase Blockchain based game which comes along with a well written tutorial on how to develop your very own game on TeamOS and utilize the Inventory and Merchant systems.

These new additions will allow a game developer to near instantly leverage the existing NFT market within the Team3D ecosystem by setting their own prices and developer fees. We'll let the free market take care of the rest.

Generator

The FPS is currently our main goal with the Generator system being worked on part-time for now. Why? For one, the Generator relies on two different systems that need to be built out and tested before the actual Generator itself. It's all unknown territory because nobody has built anything like this which means we don't really have a secure and proven base to build on. Secondly, there already exists a good way for anyone interested to stake their VIDYA in both the community driven VidyaFlux as well as providing liquidity on Uniswap the traditional way.

The goal of Team3D is and has always been to develop games. We are indie developers who truly love what we do and know best. With this in mind, you should never expect us to launch a yield farming system with unrealistic returns. If this is your main expectation from Team3D, we suggest you look elsewhere. However, for those of you who are as eager as us to play a video game and have fun, we can assure you that the Generator, once ready, will complement all of our current and future systems, but makes no promise of returns.

How it works
Players stake their VIDYA tokens and get the FLUX token in return. The price of FLUX is stable and is not affected by buys and sells. FLUX is always liquid meaning you can sell it back to VIDYA at any time.
FLUX is similar to Uniswap's native LP token in the sense that it tracks your VIDYA percentage in the pool of other liquidity providers.
There is a 5% buy and 5% sell fee that the player pays in VIDYA upon entering and exiting the game.
This fee is distributed to all FLUX holders relative to their stake percent.
Additionally 0.5% of all volume is pooled towards our Inventory contract. If you like, you may cast a magic spell to send currently pooled funds to the Inventory contract. Doing so will reward you with 1% of the total current accumulated Inventory pool.
