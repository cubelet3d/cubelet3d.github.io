Agnosia is finally live!

Fastest way to get started: 

0. connect wallet to Arbitrum mainnet
1. bridge eth at https://bridge.arbitrum.io
2. buy starter pack to get 7 cards (0.01e each) 
3. upload the cards from deck to game 
4. play practice mode
5. look for open games or create a new game 
6. win cards 
7. ascend cards 
8. brew cards (very important) 
9. get rewards
10. expect nice things 

Notes: to get the latest experience always refer to https://agnosia.gg since the version running on TeamOS might not have all the latest bells and whistles. I'm working on it, but the standalone version takes priority.
